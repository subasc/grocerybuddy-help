Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add family members?
    Go to the Master List tab and tap "Add Family Member" to invite them.
  • How do I reset my list?
    Go to Settings and select "Clear Data" to start fresh.
  • How do I track prices?
    Add the price when you purchase an item, and Grocery Buddy will store it for future comparison.
